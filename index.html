How to Duplicate One-Handed Frogpad Keyboard on standard Qwerty

As many of you know by now, Frogpad the company seems to be out of business, and the founder is AWOL. If you are anything like me, you are saddened by this and are looking for an alternative.

This is why I am releasing my frogpad script for Mac and PC. Please refer to the instructions below for your respective operating system. And don't forget to comment with anything!

Cheers! - Clark

for Windows download, AutoHotKey -> http://www.autohotkey.com/download/

once installed, download & double click on the .ahk file below and you're good to go!

Frogpad.ahk (right click, "save link as")

for Mac, download KeyRemap4MacBook -> http://pqrs.org/macosx/keyremap4macbook/

go in to system preferences > keyremap4macbook > Misc & Uninstall > open private.xml

open this in a Text Edit (textedit.app) and copy in the text from this file:

Frogpad.xml (right click, "save link as")

remember to click the "reload xml" button, exit system preferences, and you should be good to go!

After Install, How to Use:

Important! These scripts are for the Right Hand. The "J" key is remapped to the letter "H" and if you have already memorized the frogpad layout, the rest should be straightforward from there!
